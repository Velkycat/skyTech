SkyRail - CIS train simulator

About

Highly detailed cars, train stations and other locations, as well as high-quality lighting and realistic sounds will completely immerse you into the game. Explore the world in no-clip mode, walk around the cars, create your own trains in the editor without any restrictions - you are trusted with the world of SkyRail.

Released on May 23rd, 2022. Requires Android version 6.0 or up.
